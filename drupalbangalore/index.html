HISTORY

Or let us just say our Story

The Beginning

  • It all started with AnilSagar with a Meetup in May 2010.
  • The initiative was well supported by Naren, Sumeet Pareek and Arjun Raj.

The First Few

  • And they continued to organize few more meetups and then came Drupal 7 Release Party in Jan 2011 where Chakrapani joined the group!
  • Chakrapani and AnilSagar continued to organize meetups and trainings at a slower pace.

Regular Meetups

Then Gokul NK joined the party in Nov 2011. After which Gokul NK and Chakrapani continued to organize and host meetups, trainings and code sprints regularly on last saturday of every month.

The Last Saturday of Every Month

The Team Grows

  • Starting in late 2012 Safwan Erooth has conducted number of Drupal trainings as part of Global Drupal Training Days.
  • In Sep 2012, Sai Tanay moved to bangalore and became part of the community. Sai tanay has organized couple of Drupal meetups/trainings and organized a Drupal Minicamp in Sep 2013. And was a regular attendee of all Drupal meetups in 2013.

Drupal Bangalore Organizing Committe

  • During all the years there was Hussain who used to attend almost every Meetup in Bangalore and then started speaking at the meetups..and now a active member of the organizing team.
  • Mrs Jaya who was a regular attendee all the meetups started helping in organizing the meetups and inspiring the you folks and old alike.
